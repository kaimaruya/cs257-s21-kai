Why Loneliness?

After the strike of the Covid-19 pendamic, people are forced to quarantine and work from home for months. Some studies (e.g.) found that social distancing at home had some impact on people's loneliness, and this raised our interest in finding the possible relation between loneliness and other factors.

What Can We do?

This site will allow users to investigate the relationships between loneliness and isolation by generating plots for different variables, other habits and lifestyle choices, and general happiness and contentment, in order to encourage thinking about how these things impact each other and our daily lives.

What Are These Plots

This website will generate a bar plot showing how people responded to a given question, or if two questions are select will generate a heatmap, where each cell shows the probability of a respondant giving any pair of answers (e.g. yes to feeling lonely and no to feeling happy). This can help to indicate correlation and patterns in responses, though with a limited sample size and a wide variety of variables this should of course be taken with a grain of salt.
