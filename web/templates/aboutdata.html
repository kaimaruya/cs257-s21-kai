About the Data

What Is This Dataset?

This dataset contains the results of a telephone survey of Americans conducted in 2018. Participants were asked a series of questions such as "Taken all together, how would you say things are in your life these days?" and other questions about mental and social wellbeing. The variables measured include happiness, perceived loneliness, social isolation, etc.
